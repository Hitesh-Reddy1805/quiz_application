Question 2: Solve for \(x\) in \(\cos x = \frac{1}{2}\)

Step 1: Identify the general solution for \(\cos x = \frac{1}{2}\).

Step 2: The principal values of \(x\) are \(x = 60^\circ\) and \(x = 300^\circ\).

Step 3: The general solution is \(x = 60^\circ + 360^\circ k\) or \(x = 300^\circ + 360^\circ k\), where \(k \in \mathbb{Z}\).
