Solution for Question 4

Solve for \(x\) in \(\cos 2x = \frac{1}{2}\).

Step 1: Find values where \(\cos x = \frac{1}{2}\) (60° and 300°).

Step 2: Solve for \(x\): \(2x = 60° + 360°k\) or \(2x = 300° + 360°k\).

Step 3: Divide by 2 to get general solutions: \(x = 30° + 180°k\) or \(x = 150° + 180°k\).

Final Answer: \(x = 30° + 180°k, x = 150° + 180°k, k \in \mathbb{Z}\).
